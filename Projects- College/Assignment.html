Survey Results

In December 2019, faculty/staff and students at Government Engineering College were asked to complete a 17-item survey which assessed on-campus dining habits, food and beverage preferences, and satisfaction with the on-campus dining services. A total of 543 surveys were completed: faculty/ staff (n=218), students (n=261), and unknown role (n=64).
